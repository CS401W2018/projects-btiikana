What is a GPU?

Nvidia started out in 1993, initially focusing on computer graphics and gaming. Their big breakthrough came in 1999 with the release of the Geforce 256(image of the left), which was one of the first GPU(Graphics Processing Units) to handle complex graphics and 3D rendering. Their innovation changed gaming forever, allowing for smoother graphics and more immersive experiences.

Over the years, Nvidia has kept innovating, coming up with cool stuff like CUDA for better computing and RTX for real-time ray tracing, making more praphics more realistic than ever. They create GPUs not just for gamers but also for professionals in fields like AI and deep learning.
