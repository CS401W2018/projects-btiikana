Mezut Ozil

Mezut Ozil is a German soccer player and was once a regular kid with a dream. He had nothing and he came from nothing. His unwavering faith, dedication to the sport coupled with his hardwork ethics helped him fulfill his dream. He can now support his family and is highly respected in the sport he once dreamed of joining. I come from a humble beginnings and work hard to follow in his footsteps, even if our paths are different, with the same goal of overcoming struggles. Most importantly, I aspire to provide for my loved ones and earn respect in the field I aim to succeed in. Below are things that I find relevant and admire:

Turkey
He mentioned that his parents were Turkish immigrants, and that he experienced struggles growing up.
Charity
He supported education and healthcare organization for children and funded projects in his parents' home country Turkey.
FIFA World Cup 2014
His greatest accomplishment is when he helped Germany win the FIFA World Cup through his perfect assists and playmaking back in 2015.
