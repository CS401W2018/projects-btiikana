Futures

What are Futures

They're not assets or shares where you can own or hold on to forever like in Stocks Trading but Futures are contracts or agreements. The purpose of these contracts are to mark a specific date in the future (a week or month) agreed upon today for two parties to buy or sell their valuables to each other. These valuables could be Metals, Agricultural Products, Energy or Livestocks.. Futures are tradable contracts of valuables.

How do you make Money?

You simply can make money when you know how to expect price movements of the valuables, this is called Speculation. It is a calculated process that separates skilled traders from gamblers. Making money is possible when you use the right trading tools.

Levels of Trading

Depending on how you were introduced to the trading world, your skills and consistency to profit in the market can distinguish the level of your trading.

Most traders today started trading after seeing advertisements on social media or on TV. They get their training through youtube videos or from private groups and they're called Retail Traders.

While Retail Traders are polluting the trading markets and getting tricked from left to right. A group of professionals ranging from certified market analysts, risk managers to investment bankers call out shots to manipulate the prices. These groups are called Institutional Traders.

The secret and the main goal is to follow the Instutional FOOTSTEPS.

Institutional Traders
  • These entities are Bankers, Hedge Funds, Mutual Fund Managers, etc.
  • They move the market due to their high amount of trading transactions.
  • They have powerful computers.
Retail Traders
  • This group of individuals are self taught and lack formal training.
  • They have little or no influence to the price movement.
  • They use Over The Counter Trading tools to make invesment decisions.
