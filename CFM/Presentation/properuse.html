How to Use




  • Power Drill

- You usually drill holes.

- You can also fasten screws with this.

- Demonstration video link - Ace Hardware

  • Hammer Drill

- This is useful for drilling tougher materials like concrete and tough surfaces.

- It's pretty handy tool for installing anchors for shelves.

- Demonstration video link - Ace Hardware

  • Impact Drill

- You can use this to drive large screws into metals.

- It's a quick tool to assemble furnitures

- Demonstration video link - Ace Hardware
