What are Portable Electric Drills?

Portable electric drills are compact, battery-operated tools designed for drilling holes and driving screws in various materials. They are mobile which makes them convinient to carry around indoors and outdoos.

History

Then

  • First Electric Drill was invented by two Australians, Arthur Arnot & William Bryan (1889).


  • Early Cordless Drills were powered by Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) Batteries (1962)


Now

  • Before widespread adoption, Milwaukee Tool first incorporated Lithium-Ion Batteries to their tools (2005).
