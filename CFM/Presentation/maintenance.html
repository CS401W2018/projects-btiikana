Maintenance





  • Clean After Use

    - Wipe dust and inspect for any wears or debris.



  • Store in a Dry Area

    - Prevent rust and extend drill life.



  • Inspect Battery or Cord

    - Check the main components. Make sure to charge batteries after use and change out wires plug if frayed.
