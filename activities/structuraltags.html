Web Development: Structual Tags

Structural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)

1. What goes in the header?

Header(<header></header>) - Contents that are usually preferred on top of the page, and they don't change when the page is loaded.

2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Navigation(<nav></nav>) - Links to web pages are located here. They don't usually move but they might have a slight change when the "highlight" marker is displayed.

3. What goes in Main?

Main(<main></main>) - Contents inside the main part of the web page that will always change when different web pages are loaded.

4. What goes in the Footer?

Footer(<footer></footer>) - Address, Contact, Copyright, and Policy Information are usually located here.
