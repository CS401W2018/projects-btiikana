    • Questions

    The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

  • The 'action" attribute tells the browser where to send the information filled in the form and works in conjuction with a submit input.

    • The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

  • The "method" attribute tells the browser HOW to send the information filled in the form but depending on the two of the methods (GET and POST) used.

    • What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

  • The difference is "Get" is used when you're searching or filtering information while "Post" is used when changing information
